# OctoAcme Roles and Personas

This document defines key personas involved in OctoAcme project delivery and explains how they collaborate to ensure successful outcomes.

## Core Roles

### Project Manager
Responsible for planning, coordination, governance, and stakeholder communication throughout the project lifecycle.

### Delivery Team Members
Execute assigned tasks, report progress, and surface risks or issues during execution.

### Stakeholders
Provide business context, review progress, and participate in key decision-making.

## Additional Personas

### Product Owner
Owns product vision and priorities. Works closely with the Project Manager and Delivery Team to ensure requirements are clearly defined and aligned with business goals.

### Quality Lead
Ensures quality standards are defined and followed. Coordinates reviews, validation activities, and continuous improvement practices.

### Technical Architect
Provides technical direction, ensures architectural alignment, and supports delivery teams on complex technical decisions.

### Operations / Support Lead
Ensures solutions are operationally ready, supports deployment planning, and coordinates post‑delivery support.

## Collaboration Model

All personas collaborate through regular communication, documented decisions, and shared artifacts to maintain transparency, accountability, and quality.
